**Short answer.** Orange Gateway is an independent exchange in Iceland, known as a place to trade BSV for regular money. On 30 June 2026 it announced that it was not granted the license it needs (called a MiCA license) by the Central Bank of Iceland. It stopped deposits and trading right away and asked customers to withdraw all their funds within 60 days. The company is appealing; if the appeal fails, it expects to close for good.

## More detail

In the company's own words: its license application "was not fully processed due to incomplete information," it is appealing the decision, deposits and trading are stopped with immediate effect, and customers should withdraw all funds and digital assets within 60 days. If the appeal fails, the company expects to close the platform permanently.

The timing was no accident. Under Europe's new rulebook for digital-money businesses, MiCA ([[what-is-mica]]), the grace period ended on 1 July 2026. From that day, a business without the license must stop serving customers in the EU and in Iceland, Liechtenstein, and Norway (which follow the EU's market rules — that is why Iceland's central bank makes this decision). Orange Gateway was far from alone: news coverage around the deadline reported that most European digital-asset businesses did not yet have the license, affecting millions of customers across many platforms and many different coins.

What it means for you: if you have money at Orange Gateway, follow the company's withdrawal instructions within the stated period ([[exchange-closes-my-funds]]). Coins you keep in your own wallet ([[custodial-vs-own-wallet]]) are not affected by any exchange's closure. Other places to buy and sell BSV are covered in [[where-to-get-bsv]].

Who decided what: the licensing decision was made by a government regulator — the Central Bank of Iceland — about one company, Orange Gateway, which is responsible for its own paperwork and rule-following. Orange Gateway is independent: it is not owned or run by any organization in the BSV world ([[ecosystem-who-is-who]], [[what-bsva-does-not-do]]).

## What we don't know yet

- How the appeal will end, and whether the platform will close permanently.
- Exactly what information the regulator considered incomplete (only Orange Gateway's own description is public).
